    Art at Millennium Park

    11/11/14
    Written by: Samuel Navarro

    At Millennium Park, visitors will discover a state-of-the-art collection of architecture, landscape design and art that provide the backdrop for hundreds of free cultural programs including concerts, exhibitions, tours, and family activities. In Millennium Park, you’ll find a new kind of town Square – a lively, spectacular gathering spot located in the heart of the city and a destination for Chicagoans and visitors alike. In the picture, Ying Xueli, 21, paints at Millennium Park in Chicago, Illinois, on Sept. 19, 2014. Xueli is an art student at the Art Institute of Chicago. (photo by Samuel Navarro)
